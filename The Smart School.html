About

Established in the year 2012,The Smart Schoolwas launched to offer world-class, affordable education in Pakistan. Established under the aegis of The City School, it is committed to providing an efficient learning environment for your children, helping them compete and excel in the rapidly evolving environment of today. With over 400 campuses in 200 cities across Pakistan, we offer quality education from Kindergarten till FSc over 100,000 students, in efficient and well-resourced campuses with high-tech amenities.

Our Vision

“To empower the future of the nation through an affordable yet standardized quality of education”

Our Mission

To establish a network that imparts high quality education without discrimination. We aim to develop youth who are holistically groomed and empowered to meet the challenging world head-on. Employing the latest teaching techniques, our mission is to inculcate values, morals and ethics amongst our students as well as grooming them for a successful future.
